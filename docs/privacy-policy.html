Golf Weather

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026
⛳ Short version: Golf Weather does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens on your device.

1. What we collect

Nothing. Golf Weather does not collect any personal information, usage data, crash reports, analytics, or any other data from your device.

2. Location

If you grant location permission, your device's GPS coordinates are used only to look up the local weather forecast from the Open-Meteo public API. Your location is:

  • Never stored on-device beyond the current app session
  • Never transmitted to any server we operate
  • Never shared with any third party other than the Open-Meteo API (which is called anonymously with no account or identifier)

You may deny location permission at any time. The app will continue to work when you manually search for a location by name.

3. Third-party services

Golf Weather uses one external service:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — a free, open-source weather API. Requests contain only a latitude and longitude. No user identifiers are sent. Open-Meteo's own privacy policy applies to their service.

The optional "Buy me a coffee" button links to Ko-fi (ko-fi.com). Tapping it opens your browser; any data you enter on Ko-fi is governed by Ko-fi's privacy policy, not ours.

4. App settings

Your scoring preferences (wind/rain/temperature weights, preferred tee time, round length, and unit choices) are saved locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device.

5. Children

Golf Weather does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children. Because we collect no data at all, COPPA and equivalent regulations are satisfied by default.

6. Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices (for example, by adding analytics), we will update this page and increment the app version. The effective date at the top will reflect the latest revision.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@dusk-labs.com.
